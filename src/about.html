About Me

Hello! I am Prince Andrew Casiano, a dedicated Information Systems student with a strong passion for Web Development.

My journey into coding began with a curiosity about how websites work. That curiosity quickly turned into a passion for building efficient, user-friendly applications. I specialize in backend development with PHP and MySQL, but I also love crafting smooth frontend experiences using Tailwind CSS and React.

I believe that technology should solve real-world problems. Whether it's a grading system for schools or a booking platform for photographers, I aim to build software that makes life easier and more organized.
